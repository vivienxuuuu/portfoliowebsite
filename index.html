Projects

Social Media Analytics for Edinburgh Food Project

Data Analysis Facebook Instagram Content Strategy Engagement Optimization

Project Overview

Conducted comprehensive analysis of Edinburgh Food Project's social media performance across Facebook and Instagram, providing data-driven insights and recommendations to improve engagement, reach, and follower growth.

Key Findings

  • Posts with 40-60 words generated higher engagement than shorter posts
  • Strategic posting at 2 AM generated significantly higher impressions
  • Using 2-3 emojis in Instagram posts produced optimal engagement
  • Consecutive posting created synergistic effects, significantly increasing profile visits and follows
  • Identified substantial gaps between active posting periods, representing missed engagement opportunities

Recommendations

  • Implement consistent posting schedule with 3-4 consecutive posts during high-engagement periods
  • Increase post text length to 40-60 words for Facebook content
  • Schedule posts at optimal times (2 AM) to maximize reach
  • Include 2-3 emojis in Instagram posts to boost engagement
  • Increase reach through targeted promotion to grow follower base

Tools & Methods

  • Data Collection: Facebook & Instagram Analytics APIs
  • Data Analysis: Python, Excel
  • Data Visualization: Tableau
  • Metrics: Engagement rate, CTR, follower conversion rate

Results

  • Identified key optimization strategies based on historical data
  • Provided framework for measuring and improving social media performance
  • Recommendations projected to increase engagement by 30-40% if implemented
